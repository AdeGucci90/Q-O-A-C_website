    ABOUT QUEEN OF AFRICAN CONTINENT

    Distinctively the world of beauty has been known through fashion and pageantry, but history has shown beauty and pictures are the memories and aural in which beauty is really determined. People from all over the globe will be fascinated with the idea of beautiful  Confident women competing for the title of QUEEN OF AFRICAN CONTINENT. While the sash and crown have become pop culture mainstays, QUEEN OF AFRICAN CONTINENT is an international class pageant and is aimed at creating a more sustainable platform for beautiful women across Africa by projecting the true African culture and its beauty.

    OUR
    MISSION

    Queen of African Continent (QOAC) is a brand that aimed at bringing out the personal best of every woman, by helping them achieved their dreams through our platform.

    OUR
    VISION

    Our vision is to give every beautiful Africa woman the platform to showcase their hidden potential through our pageantry platform.

    OUR
    VALUE

    QUEEN OF AFRICAN CONTINENT is valued for Integrity,Intelligence Confidence and inner beauty.
